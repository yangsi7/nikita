## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-DoS-1 | Denial of Service | /login 410 before middleware redirect update | mitigate | Task 1 (middleware redirect update) ships BEFORE Task 2 (410). Rolling deploy sequence enforced by task ordering in this plan. Any client redirected to /login by old middleware hits 410 only after middleware is already pointing to TG bot. |
| T-Info-1 | Information Disclosure | /auth/bridge route deletion | accept | Bridge route contained OAuth callback logic. Deletion removes the surface. No PII persisted in bridge. |
